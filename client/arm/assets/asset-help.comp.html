Help managing assets

Adding a new asset

  1. Click the create new asset button
  2. Enter valid asset details
  3. Click save. Any error will be reported
  4. if there is no Error, the navigation pan will close and the asset can be seen in the list of assets

Deleting an asset

  1. Hover the mouse over the asset you want to delete
  2. A recycle bin icon will apear on the same line:
  3. Click the recycle bin icon, then confirm or cancel the deletion
